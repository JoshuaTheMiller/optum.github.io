Upstream Engineering is an effort to exert positive influences on those open source projects and organizations upon which Optum relies for continued software engineering success in the healthcare technology industry.  Our goal is to continually increase our participation  and support in those open source communities which have enabled our achievements on behalf of our customers and the people we serve.  We are proud of these individuals and their sustained efforts of innovation on behalf of the open source community.
